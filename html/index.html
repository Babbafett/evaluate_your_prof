Welcome

Evaluate your prof makes the quality of your lectures and the competence of your professors comparable.

By clicking "home" or our logo you will get to this site.

With the button "overall" you will see the evaluations' rating. Our descriptive diagrams give you a good overview for the results of our analysis.

With a click on "Prof" you will get to a survey of the Professors you can rate at this site. In addition you find closer information about each professor.

The last point "admin" is used only by us, so that we can administrate evaluate your prof and so continue with our service for you.

At the bottom of every page you can find a link to the contact formular.

If you have questions, annotations or suggestions on how we can improve evaluate your prove even more, please use the contact formular to contact us.
